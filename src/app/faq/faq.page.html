FAQ

  • What does Majid Barbershop provide to customers?

    It provide haircut for adult, haircut for kids and shaving

  • How to book an appointment?

    You can tap the button book an appointment on the side menu and fill in the form.

  • How to know the appointment I have booked?

    You can tap the track appointment page from sidemenu and see the ongoing appointment at the top of the list

  • Can i cancel the appointment?

    Yes, you may cancel the appointment by tapping the cancel button at the track appointment page

  • What is the payment method provided?

    The payment method that is provided for this barbershop is only cash.
