About Us

Kedai Gunting Rambut Abdul Majid In May of 1999, Abdul Majid opened their first Barber Shop that is located at Seremban, Negeri Sembilan. The Barbers was founded on the idea of reviving the old-fashioned barber shop experience and the hope that men of all ages would enjoy a haircut by Barbers who specialize in cutting only men's hair.
